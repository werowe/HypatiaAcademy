## About Hypatia Lab

Hypatia Lab teaches Arduino, electronics, computer architecture, and the C programming language.

The goal is not simply to connect sensors to an Arduino. Students learn what is happening underneath the code—from electric current and transistor switches to logic gates, binary arithmetic, memory, registers, and a simple CPU.
